Short bio

Ph.D. candidate in Electrical Engineering at New York University working on mmWave and sub-THz propagation, RF circuit design, and system-level channel sounding. I design measurement campaigns and develop calibration and metrology for high-frequency wireless research.

Research & highlights

  • FR3 and FR1C upper mid-band radio propagation measurements (6.75 GHz and 16.95 GHz).
  • Designed a THz measurement facility and four-port SOLR calibration up to 125 GHz.
  • RF circuit design: 24 GHz vector modulator phase shifter, evaluation boards for channel sounders.
